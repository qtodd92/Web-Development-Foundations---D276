Augusta, GA

Location

Augusta is a city located in Richmond County in the state of Georgia. It lies on the Savannah River, across from South Carolina

About

Weather

Pleasant summers and mild winters.

Population

Around 200,000 people call Augusta home.

Sports

Augusta is known for its golf courses and hosts the Masters golf tournament every spring.

Things To Do

  • Take a boat ride through the Augusta Canal.
  • Hike the trails of Phinizy Swamp.
  • Stroll along the Augusta Riverwalk.
